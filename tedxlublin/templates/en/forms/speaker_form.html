About Speaking at TEDxLublin

TEDxLublin provides a platform for the brightest minds, most inspiring innovators, and creative thinkers in our community to share their ideas worth spreading. Our speakers come from diverse backgrounds and disciplines, each bringing a unique perspective to our stage.

Selection Process

Our speaker selection process is rigorous and competitive. We look for:

  • Original ideas that challenge conventional thinking
  • Innovative solutions to pressing problems
  • Inspiring stories of personal or professional transformation
  • Cutting-edge research or discoveries
  • Unique artistic or cultural perspectives

Speaker Support

Selected speakers receive comprehensive support from our team, including:

  • One-on-one coaching to refine your idea and presentation
  • Guidance on creating impactful visuals
  • Rehearsal opportunities with our TEDx team
  • Professional video recording of your talk

Future Opportunities

While this year's registration is closed, we encourage you to:

  • Attend our upcoming TEDxLublin event to experience the talks firsthand
  • Follow us on social media for announcements about future speaker opportunities
  • Sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about TEDxLublin activities
  • Continue developing your ideas and practicing your public speaking skills
