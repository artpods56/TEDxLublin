JOIN US AS VOLUNTEER

It's a pleasure to see that you are interested in volunteering for TEDxLublin! We kindly ask you to fill out the form below. This is so we can get to know a little bit more about you. As a volunteer you will be tasked with helping us to make the event a success. Tasks differ from ticket scanning to assisting guests and helping out with other practical stuff. After the event we will celebrate with drinks to express gratitude for your help. We will contact you later with more information.

Thank you!

VOLUNTEER APPLICATION

Thank you for your interest in volunteering for TEDxLublin!

Please apply if you are able to commit to a full day of volunteering at the event, or are able to help with event support between now and the event.

PLEASE NOTE: Priority for day-of volunteers will be given to those who have been regularly involved in the months leading up to the event, as volunteer spots are limited. So — get involved as soon as possible if you want to experience TEDx from behind the scenes!

If you do not hear back from us right away, please be patient. We are all volunteers! You can also email volunteer@tedxlublin.com to check on the status of TEDx volunteer meetings or sign up to our email list and select "Volunteer Opportunities".
