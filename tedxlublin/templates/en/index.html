ACCELERATE 2024

Join us on this journey at TEDxLublin to explore how we can accelerate into tomorrow, today.

Learn about TED 

In an era where change accelerates faster than ever, "Acceleration" is not just an observation, but an imperative. We live in a time where technology, society, the environment, and the world of work evolve at unprecedented speeds. This acceleration varies by place, context, and perspective, yet its impact is universal, touching every aspect of our lives.

At TEDxLublin ACCELERATE, we will delve into how to thrive amidst this rapid change. Join us on September 7th to hear from visionary speakers and engage in conversations that will inspire you to adapt and flourish. Don’t miss out on being part of this transformative dialogue.
